Welcome to ShardedMC

ShardedMC is a community-driven survival server where creativity meets challenge. Enjoy a rich Minecraft experience filled with events, PvP, and a balance between freedom and fairness. Build, raid, explore, and trade in a world full of possibility.

Server Rules

1. Gameplay & Survival

  • Griefing and raiding are allowed, but excessive destruction is not.
    • Do not completely flatten bases, lava-cast structures, or leave areas unplayable.
    • Leave behind enough so players can rebuild and continue playing.
  • PvP is allowed outside of protected zones unless otherwise stated.
    • No spawn camping or constant targeting of the same player.

2. Fair Play

  • No hacked clients or unfair mods:
    • ❌ X-ray, fly hacks, kill auras, auto-clickers, duplication exploits.
    • ✅ Cosmetic/performance mods like OptiFine, minimaps (without cave/radar), and HUDs are fine.
  • No exploiting glitches, bugs, or unintended mechanics for unfair advantage.

3. Builds & World

  • Do not lag the server with large redstone clocks, spawner mobs, or chunk loaders.
  • Avoid making the world ugly near spawn:
    • No random holes, sky towers, or lava spam.
  • Public/shared spaces must be treated with respect.

4. Chat & Community

  • Be respectful — banter is welcome, but hate isn’t.
    • No racism, hate speech, harassment, or personal threats.
    • Use common sense and keep it fun.
  • No spamming or advertising external servers or links.
  • English only in global chat unless stated otherwise.

5. Spawn & Server Areas

  • Spawn and server builds are protected zones — no griefing, PvP, or trolling.
  • Use designated areas for trades, warps, events, etc.

6. Staff & Enforcement

  • Admins and moderators reserve the right to intervene at any time.
  • Breaking the rules may result in:
    • Warnings
    • Temporary bans
    • Permanent bans (for major offenses)

7. Use Common Sense

If you have to ask whether something is allowed — it probably isn’t. When in doubt, ask staff or keep it reasonable.
